FINAL REQUIREMENT SPECIFICATION DOCUMENT (FRSD)
Product: Multi-Tenant Hospital CRM Platform
Initial Tenant: VIROC
Architecture: Multi-Tenant, Integration-Ready, Hub-Spoke Model

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
This CRM platform is designed as a multi-tenant hospital growth operating system. It manages Lead to Consultation Done to Conversion lifecycle, integrates with Telephony, WhatsApp, HMS, and supports structured workflow, SLA discipline, and role-based PHI governance.
2. MULTI-TENANT ARCHITECTURE
All tables must include TenantID. Data must be isolated by TenantID in all queries. Tenant resolution methods: Subdomain (preferred), Email domain mapping, User-to-Tenant mapping. Users belong to one tenant by default. Platform Super Admin may access multiple tenants.
3. SYSTEM LAYERS
Frontend: Fully responsive (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile).
Database: TenantID enforced across all tables.
Logic: Stage enforcement, SLA rules, telephony sync, reminder engine.
Authentication: Tenant-based session resolution.
Design System: Tenant-based branding (logo, colors, app name).
4. MASTER DATA STRUCTURE
Categories include Location, Organisation, Treatment & Providers, Lead Generation, Activity & Workflow, Communication, Governance.
All masters must support Code, Name, Status, DisplayOrder, CreatedAt/By, ModifiedAt/By, Bulk Upload, Audit Trail.
5. LEAD STATUS (SINGLE FUNNEL CONTROL)
LeadStatus stages: Raw Lead Captured, Qualified, Contacted, Appointment Booked, Reminder Running, Consultation Done, Closed Won, Closed Lost, Unqualified, Nurture.
Fields: Sequence, IsTerminal, IsBusinessAchieved, RequiresNextTask, AllowNurtureOption, DefaultOwnerRole, RequiredFieldsJSON.
6. PHONE ARCHITECTURE
All phone records must store PhoneType, CountryCode, PhoneNational, PhoneE164.
Primary mapping key: PhoneE164.
Mobile preferred for deduplication. Landline does not auto-merge patients.
7. TELEPHONY CONNECTOR (TOOL-AGNOSTIC)
TelephonyConnector master must allow integration with any dialer.
TelephonyCallLog must store ExternalCallID, Line, User, ClientPhoneE164, Direction, Duration, CallStatus.
Auto-create ActivityLog from call logs.
8. LEAD INGESTION
Supported sources: Meta, Google, Website Forms, Landing Pages, Google Forms, Camps, Telephony inbound.
All inbound leads must land in LeadStatus = Raw Lead Captured.
Auto-create first contact SLA task.
9. CAMPAIGN & ATTRIBUTION
Campaign object must track Platform, Channel, Budget, Start/End dates.
Lead must capture UTM fields, CampaignID, Adset, Ad, Keyword.
Dashboards must report leads and Consultation Done by campaign.
10. HMS INTEGRATION (LIGHTWEIGHT)
CRM Patient is non-clinical. HMS is clinical + billing.
Store HMSPatientID and link status.
Sync OPD visits and consultation completion status.
11. SLA & REMINDER ENGINE
SLA Rule Master: TriggerEvent, TimeLimitMinutes, AppliesToRole, EscalationRole.
ReminderPolicy: Appointment reminder offsets and channel priority.
12. TRANSACTION LOGIC
Each Lead interaction consists of:
1) Activity Log (what was done)
2) Next Action Task (what must happen next)
Next task mandatory unless status is Closed Won, Closed Lost, or Unqualified.
13. REPORTS & DASHBOARDS
Marketing Dashboard: Raw Leads by Source/Campaign.
Conversion Dashboard: Stage-to-stage conversion and drop-offs.
ROI Dashboard: Cost per Consultation Done.
Branch Dashboard: Appointments, No-show rate, SLA compliance.
14. CUSTOMER CARE (PROVISION)
SupportTicket module provisioned but advanced automation deferred.
Tickets linkable to Patient/Lead/Appointment.
15. BULK UPLOAD & AUDIT
All masters must support bulk upload with validation, duplicate handling, and audit logging.
