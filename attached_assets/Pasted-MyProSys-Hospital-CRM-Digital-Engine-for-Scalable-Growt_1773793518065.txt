1. Background and Context
Over the past several months, RGB and the VIROC leadership team have worked closely to understand the hospital’s growth vision, operational model, and patient journey.
During this process, multiple CRM solutions were evaluated. However:
• Some systems were too complex for hospital teams to adopt
• Some were very expensive for long-term use
• Others lacked the maturity required for a specialty hospital
Hospitals operate on a continuous patient journey that spans from inquiry to consultation, treatment, surgery, post-care, and referrals.
Since no single solution supported this complete journey effectively, RGB designed and built a dedicated system.
The result is the MyProSys Hospital CRM — a structured platform that tracks and manages the entire patient journey from first contact to long-term engagement and referrals.
The system is already operational and under testing with the VIROC team.
The current phase focuses on stabilization, refinement, and full-scale implementation.
2. Vision Behind the CRM
The core idea is simple:
Every patient interaction should be visible, trackable, and actionable.
Hospitals receive inquiries from multiple channels:
Digital Campaigns
Referrals
Webinars and Education Programs
Camps and Outreach Activities
Telecalling and Inbound Calls
Without a structured system:
Follow-Ups Get Missed
Visibility Is Lost
Conversion Opportunities Reduce
The MyProSys Hospital CRM creates a single structured system connecting:
Marketing → Patient Engagement → Clinical Journey → Post-Care → Referral Growth
This ensures consistency, accountability, and measurable growth.
